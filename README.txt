## Goal Summary
This project explore and compare the pattens that people accept different coupons.

## How to Run
1. Install required libraries: pandas, seaborn, etc.
2. Open and run the Jupyter notebook `coupon_acceptance.ipynb`.

##Main Findings
People with different ages or genders have different coupon acceptance preference. For example, Bar coupon is more popular among Age 26, while Restaurant coupon and Carry out & Take away are more popular among young people. Male is easier to accept the Bar coupon, while female is easier to accept the Less Expensive Restaurant coupon. 
And weather, marital status and other factors can also affect coupon acceptance rate.
